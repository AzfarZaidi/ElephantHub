Elephant Hub

Asian Elephant: 18-22 months African bush elephant: 22 months

A female elephant can give birth to as many as 12 calves.

Baby elephants are born nearly blind but they rely on their trunks and mothers for everything, their mums will usually nurse them for four to six years

They are found most often in savannas, grasslands, and forests, but they occupy a wide range of habitats, including deserts, swamps, and highlands in tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.
